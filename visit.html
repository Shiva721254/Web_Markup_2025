Choose a simple loop

Begin at the main entrance sign, then follow clear path lines that border lawns and small water edges. Keep right on narrow sections and pass kindly. For new cyclists or skaters, pick quiet times and practise short laps. If you bring a dog, keep it under control and use bins along the route. Try the same loop at sunrise and again at sunset to experience different light and sounds. Families often enjoy timing a lap and watching gradual improvement week by week.

Tip Clockwise for morning light; anticlockwise for warmer evening tones.

Navigation is straightforward: at each junction, continue on the outer edge to remain on your chosen loop. If you bring children, agree a meeting point in case someone speeds ahead. For cyclists and skaters, gloves help on cool or windy days; for walkers, waterproof shoes are useful after rain. Respect wildlife and fellow visitors: do not feed animals, keep music low and leave space when passing others. A steady routine builds familiarity with Dutch seasons and creates healthy family time outdoors.
