About Us

Our Story

Beatrix Park is one of the city’s most beloved green spaces — a place where nature, relaxation, and community come together. Established to offer residents and visitors a peaceful retreat, the park features shady trees, flowering gardens, and calm walking paths. Over time, it has grown into a vibrant meeting point for families, joggers, and nature lovers of all ages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to keep Beatrix Park clean, safe, and welcoming for everyone. We collaborate with volunteers, local gardeners, and the municipality to preserve its natural beauty and biodiversity. From sustainable planting to eco-friendly maintenance, every effort supports a greener city and a healthier future for all who enjoy this park.
